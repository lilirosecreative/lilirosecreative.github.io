ABOUT

Graphic design has allowed me to explore my creativity in new ways and develop my skills in many different areas. From my time at Murdoch university, I have learnt how powerful graphic design can be. We as designers have the ability to create exciting and innovative outputs that can change human behaviour, a skill that is something quite special. Through my work, I want to create change. I want to make people excited about design and how it may impact our world in the future.

HOW CAN I HELP YOU?

Armed with knowledge and expertise in Adobe programs such as Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, and XD, I can create unique, meaningful, and eye-catching designs. I can apply these designs to different areas that I am competent and skilled in, such as

  • Branding
  • Website design and development
  • App design
  • Packaging design
  • Publication design
  • Service design
  • Drawing
  • Digital Art
